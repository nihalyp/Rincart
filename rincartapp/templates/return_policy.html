Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to love what you ordered! If it’s not the perfect fit, we’ve made our returns and exchanges completely hassle-free.

Our Policy Guidelines

10-Day Window

Items can be returned or exchanged within 10 days of the delivery date tracking timestamp.

Item Condition

Please keep items unworn, unwashed, and intact with all original tags and packaging preserved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Go to your Order History panel, select the order item you want to return, and click the "Request Return/Exchange" button. Follow the prompts to select a pickup window.

Once our warehouse team receives and inspects the item (typically 2-3 business days), your refund will be processed back to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Need Assistance?

Our support team is available 24/7 to help guide you through sizing questions or return logistics.
